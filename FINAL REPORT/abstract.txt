In our society eletronics play a crucial role in everyday life. From smartphones to simple lightbulbs, technology can be found everywhere. As such, the topic
of electronic e-waste is becoming more and more important, as the amount of discarded and obselete electronics is steadily increasing. 

Currently most of the e-waste in the first world is exported to third world countries instead of recycling it. 
A major cause for this, is the lack of recycling oppurtunities in the associated countries and the absence of initiative to do so. 

We propose a ingredient list similar to food items for electronic devices. This ensures a foundation to successfully collect and recycle e-waste.
This ingredient list will be released by the products' companies, which showcases a list of all raw resources used in making the product.
